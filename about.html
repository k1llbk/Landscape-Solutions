About Landscape Solutions

Transforming Outdoor Spaces Across Botswana

Who We Are

Landscape Solutions is a professional landscaping company dedicated to creating beautiful, functional and sustainable outdoor environments for residential, commercial and public spaces throughout Botswana.

Our expertise includes landscape design, irrigation systems, garden installation, turf management, hardscaping, grounds maintenance and large-scale landscaping developments.

We are passionate about transforming outdoor spaces into environments that enhance property value, improve aesthetics and provide long-term environmental benefits.
